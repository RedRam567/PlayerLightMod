Adds a dim light around the player, like the one mobile Terraria used to have.

[hr]
[h1]Settings[/h1]
[list]
[*] Client brightness (Cannot be higher than server)
[*] Server brightness (Clients can choose lower brightness)
[*] Color
[/list]
